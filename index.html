openpatata

[tentative] openpatata is an informal Cypriot initiative with a focus on improving civic access to public- and privately-held information. We hold that every person should have unencumbered and equal access to the information that shape their life and their community, free of antipopular bias. openpatata sprang in August 2015 of a desire to keep track of the business of the Cypriot parliamentâ€”what would've been a daunting task for the uninitiated. The original openpatata was a parliament monitor in the likeness of openparliament.ca and TheyVoteForYou (Australia). It has since evolved into an umbrella project for all the work we've conducted on open data.
