openpatata

[tentative] openpatata is an informal Cypriot initiative with a focus on improving civic access to publicly- and privately-held information. We hold that every person should have unencumbered and equal access to the information that shape their life and their community, free of antipopular bias. openpatata sprang in August 2015 of a desire to shed light on the business of the Cypriot parliamentâ€”what would've been a daunting task for the uninitiated. The original openpatata was a parliament monitor in the likeness of openparliament.ca and TheyVoteForYou (Australia). It has since evolved into an umbrella project for all the work we've conducted on open data.

Work

  • openpatata-data

    Machine-readable parliament data

    The data are harvested from plenary transcript PDFs and the official parliament website. They include plenary sittings, questions and MPs.

  • someone has to do this

    Discourse analysis around open data in Cyprus

    The work was presented at the nEUROsis exhibition in Limassol in November 2016. It critically examines the notion of the state as a hoarder of information that is impeding economic development.

  • neavouli

    Platform for crowdsourcing candidate data

    neavouli was created in the run-up to the 2016 legislative election. It contains volunteer-contributed biographical and other information on all candidates in the election. Data is available for download in the CSV format and through a JSON API.

  • erotiseis

    MP question web interface

    erotiseis presents questions MPs posed to the executive in a searchable format. Visualisations are in the pipeline.

  • foi

    Freedom of Information request platform

    A platform through which people will be able to request information from local and national government bodies. Questions and answers are logged and are made publicly available.
